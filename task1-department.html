Department of Computing and Informatics

Quality education in computing and technology - preparing you for the digital world

The Department of Computing and Informatics is dedicated to providing quality education in computing and technology. Our programs are designed to prepare students for successful careers in software development, data science, cybersecurity, and other technology fields.

We offer state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and hands-on learning opportunities. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills to ensure our graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern tech industry.
