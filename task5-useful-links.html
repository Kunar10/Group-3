📋 Admission Requirements

  • Undergraduate: High school diploma or equivalent with a minimum GPA of 2.0 in science and mathematics
  • Postgraduate: Bachelor's degree with a minimum GPA of 3.0 in a related field
  • Diploma: High school diploma or equivalent
  • Certificate: High school diploma or proven relevant work experience

✨ Programme Highlights

  • Accredited: All programmes are internationally recognized and accredited
  • Industry-Relevant: Curriculum developed with input from technology industry partners
  • Hands-on Learning: State-of-the-art labs equipped with modern technologies
  • Experienced Faculty: Instructors with industry experience and advanced qualifications
  • Career Services: Job placement assistance and internship opportunities
  • Flexible Options: Full-time, part-time, and online learning modes available
