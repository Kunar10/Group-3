About Our Department

A legacy of academic excellence since 1948 shaping minds and building futures in computing

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible computing education that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and ethical practice preparing graduates for successful careers and meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computing and informatics education in East Africa, producing graduates who are innovative, ethical, and globally competitive in the technology industry.

Our History

Bugema University was founded in 1948 by the Seventh-day Adventist Church. The Department of Computing and Informatics was established to meet the growing demand for technology professionals in Uganda and the East African region. Over the decades, we have grown into one of Uganda's most respected computing departments, graduating thousands of successful alumni who have made significant contributions across technology, business, government, and public service.
