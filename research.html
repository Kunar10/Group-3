Research

Advancing knowledge through innovation, collaboration, and cutting-edge computing research

Applied Research Innovation Hub Industry Partnerships

The Department of Computing and Informatics is committed to producing impactful research that addresses real-world challenges in Uganda and the broader East African region. Our faculty and students collaborate on projects spanning artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering, and more.

Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Developing intelligent systems for healthcare, agriculture, and public services in the Ugandan context. Projects include crop disease detection and medical diagnosis support tools.

Cybersecurity

Researching threats, vulnerabilities, and defenses relevant to East African digital infrastructure. Focus areas include mobile banking security and government systems protection.

Data Science & Analytics

Applying data-driven approaches to education, health, and business sectors. Current projects include student performance prediction and health data analytics.

Software Engineering

Investigating best practices for software development in resource-constrained environments, including agile methodologies and low-bandwidth application design.

Human-Computer Interaction

Designing accessible and usable technology for diverse African users, including research on local language interfaces and accessibility for users with disabilities.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Exploring smart systems for agriculture, smart cities, and environmental monitoring. Projects include soil moisture sensors and smart irrigation systems.

Ongoing — 2026

E-Health Records System
